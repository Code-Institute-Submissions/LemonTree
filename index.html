About LemonTree

Here at LemonTree our goal is to cut through the noise and provide the consumer with the purest and most delicious water alternative out there.

We care about your health.

To see one of the world's most classic beverages re-imagined, we've searched all over to bring you a lemonade made from ingredients that are all native to their lands of origin.

LEMON

All of our lemons are sourced from Italy's very own Amalfi Coast, home to the most bountiful lemon groves in the world.

COCONUT

We use only the finest coconut sugar found in the Phillipines. Rich in both minerals and antioxidants lacking in other sweetners, we find coconut sugar to be a healthier alternative.

WATER

Only the purest Icelandic spring water is allowed into every bottle giving our lemonade a high PH and keeping it well balanced.
